FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about co-hosting and short-term rental licensing.

What is co-hosting?
Co-hosting is a service where we manage your short-term rental, including guest communication, bookings, pricing, and daily operations while you remain the owner.
Do I still own my property and listing?
Yes. You remain the full owner of your property and listing; we manage and optimize it on your behalf.
Which platforms do you manage?
We manage listings on major platforms like Airbnb, VRBO, Booking.com and other relevant channels.
How does your pricing work?
We offer commission-based management plans and a flat-fee trial. Our success is aligned with your booking performance.
Can I still use my property?
Yes. You have full control of your calendar and can block dates anytime.
Do you handle cleaning and maintenance?
Yes. We coordinate cleaning, turnovers, and maintenance to keep your property guest-ready.
Do I need a rental license?
In most areas, yes. We help guide you through the licensing process and ensure compliance.
How do I get started?
Contact us to schedule a consultation, and we’ll create a plan tailored to your property.
